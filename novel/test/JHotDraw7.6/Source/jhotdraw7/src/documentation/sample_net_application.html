JHotDraw 7: Net Sample Application

Net is a demonstration of a simple graph editor, consisting of only two types of figures: NodeFigure and ConnectionFigure.

Launch JHotDraw Net:

Figure 1: Net Screenshot. Click image for larger view:

Each NodeFigure has four connection points which can be used to link it to other nodes.
NodeFigures are connected using ConnectionFigures.
